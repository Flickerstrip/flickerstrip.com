Create your own light show, just the way you want it Flickerstrip is the LED light strip that lets you upload custom, animated patterns or "lightworks". The addressable neopixel light strip means that you can set each pixel to one of 16 million colors. Build your own peronsonalized sequences and light shows to liven up any party and decorate your life with light.

Try the lightwork designer out for yourself!
Control LED strips with your phone or computer via Wi-Fi
Configure existing patterns or create your own
Leverage a powerful API to script your own behavior
Decorate your life with vibrant colors
Control every pixel to create patterns with your own personal touch
Share your patterns and download popular patterns shared by others

Features

The LED strip has evolved

Connects to your home

A quick setup process connects Flickerstrip to your Wi-Fi network making it easy to take control

16M Colors

Control each pixel independently with a palette of one of 16 million colors for smooth transitions

Build your own patterns

Choose one of the many premade patterns or build your own unique patterns from scratch with the graphical interface

Connect and share

Browse through patterns submitted by other users or upload your own light creations for all to see

Cross-platform

Use the app from your desktop or phone with apps for Windows, OSX, Android, iOS, and Linux

Synchronize the show

Configure multiple strips and create groups to create a synchronized show

Lightwork Designer

The key element of the Flickerstrip app is the powerful Lightwork Designer interface that lets you build your own custom patterns from scratch using a graphical interface. Adjust the repetition, frame rate, and colors and then let your creativity take over as you paint your own light creations.

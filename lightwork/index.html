Lightwork Editor

This is the Flickerstrip lightwork editor, using this tool you can build your own lightworks to upload to your Flickerstrip. This web-based version allows you to edit works, but cannot upload them to your Flickerstrip due to browser security restrictions. To upload patterns to your Flickerstrip, download the smartphone app or desktop application.
